Module: Digital Technologies

Module Leader: Chandranna Rayadurg

This module explores the impact of innovation and digital technologies on organisational improvements and development. It also considers the impact of data and knowledge management for analysing business decision-making and allows you to identify improvements for innovation and growth using analysis of information and data.

This module aims to develop studentsâ€™ knowledge of approaches to innovation and digital technologies and their impact on organisations. Students will identify service/organisational improvements and opportunities for innovation and growth, using qualitative and quantitative analysis of information and data benchmarking.

Upon successful completion of the module the students will be able to:

  • LO1: Critically evaluate the impact of innovation and digital technologies on organisations.
  • LO2: Determine the use of innovation and digital technologies on organisational development.
  • LO3: Analyse the impact of innovation and digital technologies on data and knowledge management for analysing business decision-making.
  • LO4: demonstrate the use of qualitative and quantitative analysis of information to identified service/organisational improvement and opportunities for innovation and growth.
